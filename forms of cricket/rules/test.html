Test Cricket Rules


General Rules:

  • The umpires are now able to use their discretion as to whether they report breaches of the following Law 42 offences:
    1. A bowler repeatedly bowling more than the allowed number of bouncers above shoulder height in an over.
    2. A bowler repeatedly bowling full tosses above waist height that are likely to inflict injury.
    3. A bowler repeatedly running on the danger area on the pitch.
    4. Stealing a run
